International Bank Account Validator

This international bank account validator form automatically sets the validation pattern (regex) and help text for the IBAN and BIC input fields based on the selected country.
These widgets leverage the client-side validation support of modern browsers. Javascript is used to dynamicly update the pattern and title attributes.

This client-side International Bank Account Validator is a concept by Jasper Moelker. The IBAN patterns used for validation are adapted from the php-iban registry file. Part of the form styles are borrowed from Twitter Bootstrap.
